Struct MarketDepth2

A structure to represent "Small" and "Large" best bids and asks. Often best bids and asks have very small volume, which could create noise. "Small/Large" price is a WAP price we will pay/receive to buy/sell "Small/Large" quantity. The values are constructed from a limit order book snapshots to simplify modeling of historical market depth, reduce memory and increase speed. The notion of "Small/Large" depends on the context. Offset is the number of minimum price steps from absolute best bid/ask. Allows to recover those absolute best bid/ask levels with known price step or estimate market impact.
