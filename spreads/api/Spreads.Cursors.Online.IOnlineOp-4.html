Interface IOnlineOp<TKey, TValue, TResult, TCursor>

A state machine that maintains a state of an online algorithm over a span and reacts to addition of new values and removal of old value. A span is defined by a pair of left and right cursors (inclusive) over a series. The cursors could move independently, but the right cursor must be ahead of or at the same place as the left one.
