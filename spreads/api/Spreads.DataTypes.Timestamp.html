Struct Timestamp

A Timestamp stored as nanos since Unix epoch as Int64. 2^63: 9,223,372,036,854,780,000 Nanos per day: 86,400,000,000,000 (2^47) Nanos per year: 31,557,600,000,000,000 (2^55) 292 years of nanos in 2^63 is ought to be enough for everyone (except JavaScript).
