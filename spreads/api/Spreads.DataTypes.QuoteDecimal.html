Remarks

Price cannot be floating point, but decimal is slow and fat 16 bytes, plus such precision is not needed. Volume is in price units, 4B lots is a lot. Tag is often needed to store additional data.

This struct could represent a single or aggregate order at level in an order book, tag allows to lookup additoinal info without polluting and slowing down order book. Tag is application-specific. When not used, it has zero cost when data is serialized and compressed. When is memory, it guarantees alignment of Price field that could make processing somewhat faster.
