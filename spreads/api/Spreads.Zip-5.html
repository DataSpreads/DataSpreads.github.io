Struct Zip<TKey, TLeft, TRight, TCursorLeft, TCursorRight>

A cursor that inner-joins two series. Each continuous series is evaluated at every key of another series. To get full outer join make each series continuous by applying a transformation such as Fill<TKey, TValue, TCursor> or RepeatWithKey<TKey, TValue, TCursor>.
